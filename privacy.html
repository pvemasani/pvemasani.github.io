Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Covered Calls Ledger ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our options trading tracking service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information (name, email address)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our trading ledger service
  • Track and analyze your trading performance

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. All data is encrypted and stored securely.

Financial Data Protection

As a financial tracking service, we maintain strict security protocols to protect your trading data and personal information.
